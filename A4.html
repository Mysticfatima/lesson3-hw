The picture you're seeing above is a place called 'Jamburi Park'.

Jamburi Park in Chittagong city, Bangladesh, located near the bustling Agrabad area, is a popular recreational spot for both locals & visitors. It features expansive green spaces, walking paths & playgrounds, offering a peaceful escape from the city's busy life. Families often visit to enjoy outdoor activities, and the park is known for hosting various events & fairs.

It's central location makes it a convenient destination for relaxation and leisure amidst the urban environment of Chittagong.
